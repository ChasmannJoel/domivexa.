General Questions

What is digital marketing?

Digital marketing encompasses all marketing efforts that use electronic devices and the internet. This includes SEO, social media marketing, email marketing, content marketing, PPC advertising, and more. It helps businesses connect with their target audience online and achieve their business goals.

Why do I need digital marketing for my business?

Digital marketing is essential because that's where your customers are. With billions of people using the internet daily, digital marketing helps you reach a wider audience, target specific demographics, track results in real-time, and achieve a better return on investment compared to traditional marketing methods.

How is Domivexa different from other digital marketing agencies?

We focus on building long-term partnerships with our clients. We provide personalized strategies tailored to your specific business goals, transparent reporting, and dedicated account management. Our team stays updated with the latest digital marketing trends and algorithm changes to ensure your campaigns remain effective.

What industries do you work with?

We work with businesses across various industries including e-commerce, professional services, healthcare, hospitality, technology, retail, and more. Our strategies are adaptable to different sectors and business sizes, from startups to established enterprises.

Contracts & Commitments

Is there a minimum contract period?

Yes, most of our service packages require a minimum 3-month commitment. Digital marketing strategies need time to develop and show results. This minimum period allows us to implement comprehensive strategies and optimize campaigns effectively.

Can I cancel my contract early?

You can terminate services with 30 days' written notice. However, early termination during the minimum commitment period may incur termination fees as specified in your service agreement. We're always happy to discuss any concerns before you decide to cancel.

What happens after the minimum term?

After the initial commitment period, contracts typically continue on a rolling monthly basis. You can continue with the same package, upgrade, downgrade, or cancel with 30 days' notice.

Results & Reporting

How quickly will I see results?

Timeline varies by service. PPC campaigns can show results within days, while SEO typically takes 3-6 months to show significant improvements. Social media and content marketing build momentum over time. We provide realistic timelines during our initial consultation.

Do you guarantee specific results?

While we can't guarantee specific rankings or exact results (no reputable agency can), we do guarantee our commitment to delivering quality work, following best practices, and working diligently towards your goals. We focus on sustainable, long-term growth rather than quick fixes.

How do you report on campaign performance?

We provide regular reports based on your package (monthly, bi-weekly, or weekly). Reports include key metrics, progress towards goals, insights, and recommendations. You'll also have access to a dashboard for real-time performance monitoring.

What metrics do you track?

We track metrics relevant to your specific goals, which may include website traffic, search rankings, conversion rates, click-through rates, engagement rates, lead generation, ROI, and more. We focus on metrics that directly impact your business objectives.
